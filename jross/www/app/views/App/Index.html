How We Met

It happened to be the last game of the 2012 World Series when we first met where we found ourselves at the same bar to watch all the action. We were introduced by our mutual friend who invited us to come out. We hit it off almost immediately- that is until after Josh stopped smelling Eunice’s hair.

The Proposal

It was a warm July day in Park City Utah when Josh planned a hike to Lake Blanche, a beautiful mountaintop lake where the trail climbs over 2,700 feet in 3 miles. Needless to say, Eunice was not a fan. There were talks that she'd received a "snicker bar" as a reward from the grueling hike. In the end, there was a memorable proposal complete with a picturesque backdrop.
